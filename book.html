Create authentic Korean kimchi at home with our beginner-friendly Kimchi Fermentation Kit. This kit contains everything you need to start fermenting fresh vegetables safely while enjoying delicious probiotic-rich food.

What's Included
  • ✔ Fermentation Glass Jar (1L)
  • ✔ Organic Kimchi Spice Mix
  • ✔ Airlock Lid System
  • ✔ Fermentation Weight
  • ✔ Beginner Fermentation Guide

Fermented foods are rich in probiotics that support gut health, improve digestion, and boost immunity. Our Kimchi Fermentation Kit helps beginners easily prepare delicious kimchi at home using traditional fermentation techniques.

This kit is designed with food-grade materials and includes a step-by-step guide so you can confidently start your fermentation journey.

  1. Chop and prepare fresh vegetables.
  2. Mix vegetables with the included kimchi spice blend.
  3. Place mixture inside fermentation jar.
  4. Attach airlock lid to release fermentation gases.
  5. Leave for 3-5 days for natural fermentation.
